Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

1. Introduction

BMI AI ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Health Data: Height, weight, age, and gender that you input into the calculator
  • BMI Calculations: Your calculated BMI values and analysis history
  • Chat History: Messages and conversations you have with the AI assistant
  • Preferences: Your theme preference (dark/light mode)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Calculate your BMI and provide health analysis
  • Provide AI-powered responses through Pollination AI
  • Store your calculation history locally on your device
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the extension

4. Data Storage

Local Storage Only: All your personal health data, BMI calculations, and chat history are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not transmit, store, or retain this data on our servers.

API Calls: When you request AI analysis or chat, your message is sent to Pollination AI's servers to generate responses. Please review Pollination AI's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

5. Third-Party Services

Our extension uses Pollination AI for text generation and analysis. When you use these features, your prompts are sent to Pollination AI. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy at https://pollinations.ai.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the extension
  • Delete your history at any time using the "Clear History" button
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data
  • Opt-out of data collection by not using the extension

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.
